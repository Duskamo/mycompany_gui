Company Info

We're in the business of developing internet applications to our clients. As Black Box Web Designs we specialize in web and mobile development related to rental type businesses along the panhandle in Florida. We integrate your application with the top rental services like VRBO, Homeaway, and Airbnb. We also setup booking throughout our customers sites so that your clients can book their vacation plans directly on your site, doing away with rental fee's that you would normally have to pay to the various rental services.

Even though we specialize in developing rental websites, the associates at Florida Rental Technologies are very skilled and technical in all areas of web and mobile development. We have much experience in other areas and love creating any type of software that fits our customers needs.
